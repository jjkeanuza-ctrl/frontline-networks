// Who We Are

About Frontline

Built by gamers who wanted something better — a community that puts the player experience first, always.

// Our Mission

Frontline Networks was founded with one goal: build the gaming community we always wished existed. No pay-to-win. No toxic gatekeeping. Just high-quality servers, fair moderation, and a place where every player — new or veteran — feels genuinely welcome.

We believe roleplay is at its best when the environment is stable, the rules are clear, and the staff actually care. That's what we're building here, one server at a time.

// Ground Rules

How We Operate

A few non-negotiables that keep Frontline Networks a great place for everyone.

  • [01] Respect every player regardless of rank, role, or experience level.
  • [02] No cheating, hacking, exploiting bugs, or using third-party tools to gain an advantage.
  • [03] Roleplay seriously — RDM, NLR breaks, and metagaming are not tolerated.
  • [04] Follow staff instructions. If you believe a decision is wrong, appeal it — don't argue in-game.
  • [05] Keep chat and voice comms clean. Hate speech, slurs, and harassment will result in a permanent ban.
  • [06] Have fun — that's what this is all for.
